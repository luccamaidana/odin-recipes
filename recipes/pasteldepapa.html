Pastel de papa

Recipe of pastel de papa

A very tasty recipe

For all types of seasons

Ingredients:

Filling:

  • 750g of ground meat
  • 2 onions
  • olive oil
  • 3 green onions
  • celery branch
  • carrot
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • salt,pepper,ground chili, paprika
  • a glass of white wine

Pur√©:

  • 750g of potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 spoons of butter
  • 1/2 spoon of nutmeg
  • olive oil
  • milk
  • grating cheese

Steps:

  1. Brown the minced meat in olive oil for a few minutes and add the chopped carrot, garlic, bell pepper, celery, greens and onion. Season and season. Deglaze with white wine and let the alcohol evaporate.
  2. For the puree, boil the potatoes in water and salt, mash with egg, butter and nutmeg, milk and a splash of olive.
  3. In a baking dish, make a puree base, cover with the filling, cover again with puree and finish with grated cheese.
  4. Grating in the oven
  5. Wait until it is cool, and you are ready to enjoy a Pastel de papa from Argentina
