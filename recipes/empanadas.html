Empanadas argentinas

Recipe of empanadas argentinas

This recipe is an insignia for argentinian people

It is prepared on patriotic dates, like 25 de mayo or 9 de julio and on

Ingredients:

  • a package of dough for fried empanadas
  • 500g of ground meat
  • 2 onions
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs
  • 150g of olives
  • 50g of raisins
  • 10g of cumin
  • beef fat

Steps:

  1. Cook the ground meat and chopped onions with some oil. Add salt, peper and cumin
  2. Stir gently until the meat is cooked. Then remove it from the fire
  3. Prepare a dozen of dough and put a spoon of the mixture on each cover
  4. Add some raisins and olives, don't forget some chop egg
  5. When ready, fold them and repulgue
  6. Put the beef fat to heat, and when it is melted start friying the empanadas
  7. When you see a gold colour, it is the moment of removing them
  8. Wait until they cool, and you are ready to enjoy a typical empanada from Argentina
