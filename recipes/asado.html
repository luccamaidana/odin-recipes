Asado

Recipe of asado

This recipe is a tradition to do it every sundays

It is prepared to share a moment with family

Ingredients:

  • 3kg of tapa de asado
  • 1kg of pechito de cerdo
  • 4-5 chorizos argentinos
  • 1-2 morcillas
  • grill salt

Steps:

  1. Turn on the fire and wait to heat the coal
  2. Put heated coal under the barbecue and clean it
  3. Put grill salt on the meat (the ingredients in kg, you don't want to put salt on the chorizos)
  4. Put all the meat over the barbecue, first to the fat side touching the grill
  5. Some people put cardboard over the meat, intending to loose minimum heat
  6. When the fat is melting and squirting liquid, you should see the meat to turn it around, else keep it cooking
  7. When you see a gold colour, it is the moment of remove the meat on to a roasting pan
  8. And that's it. You are ready to enjoy a typical asado from Argentina
